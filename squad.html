The squad

A football team lines up across four broad units. Below is a guide to each position, followed by some of the club's most celebrated players.

Positions on the pitch

  • Goalkeeper

    The last line of defence, the only player allowed to handle the ball inside the box.

  • Defenders

    Centre-backs and full-backs who protect the goal and start attacks from the back.

  • Midfielders

    The engine room — they link defence and attack, control tempo and press opponents.

  • Forwards

    Attackers whose main job is to create chances and score goals.
