A short history of the Reds

Liverpool FC was formed in 1892 after a dispute at Anfield, and has since grown into one of football's most decorated clubs. The timeline below highlights a few defining eras.

Key moments

  1. 1892: The club is founded by John Houlding and plays its first season.
  2. 1901: Liverpool win their first Football League title.
  3. 1960s–70s: Manager Bill Shankly transforms the club into a major force.
  4. 1977: First European Cup is won, beginning a period of continental dominance.
  5. 2005: The “Miracle of Istanbul” — Liverpool come back from 3–0 down to win the Champions League.
  6. 2019: A sixth European Cup is lifted in Madrid.
  7. 2020: A first league title in 30 years, won as Premier League champions.
