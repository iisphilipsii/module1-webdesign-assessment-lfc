Welcome to Anfield

Founded in 1892, Liverpool FC is one of the most successful and widely supported clubs in world football. Based at Anfield in the city of Liverpool, England, the club plays in the Premier League and is famous for its passionate supporters and the anthem “You'll Never Walk Alone.”

Club at a glance

  • Founded: 3 June 1892
  • Stadium: Anfield (capacity ~61,000)
  • Nickname: The Reds
  • Home colours: Red shirts, red shorts, red socks
  • Motto: You'll Never Walk Alone
